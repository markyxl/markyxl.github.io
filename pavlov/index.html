SWAT4 Enverstar Power Plant

Game version: Shack RC
Gamemodes: SND, TDM, DM, TTT?, OITC, GG...
Release date: 2. 11. 2022
Size: 45 MB
Download: here
Unique features:
- breakable windows
- improved doors
- system of vents
Description: Unlike other SWAT4 maps, this one is significantly larger with less close-quarters encounters. CTs (SWAT) start outside of the power plant and need to defend two generator rooms A and B. There are vents that lead to the bomb sites which players can use to their advantage. Doors have been improved since A-Bomb to be grabbed actually by the handle instead of the center. And there's no better feeling than sniping someone through one of the many breakable windows on this map.

SWAT4 A-Bomb Nightclub

Game version: Shack RC
Gamemodes: SND, TDM, DM, TTT?, OITC, GG...
Release date: 9. 10. 2022
Size: 29 MB
Download: here
Unique features:
- openable doors which you actually have to push/pull
- 137 breakable glass bottles
- glass breaking leaderboard
Description: Originally a mission where a firefight broke out between two gangs in a nightclub. Repurposed for Pavlov SND where CTs spawn in the back alley and Ts go through front entrance. One bomb spawn is on main stage, while the second one in a warehouse.
There are interactive doors on this map which are opened by actually pulling the door handle (HL Alyx style). This enables some interesting mechanics, like peeking through doors and shooting with second hand, opening doors for your teammates, trying to hold doors against the enemy team etc....
There are also 137 breakable glass bottles that respawn with each round. They are fun to break and there's even a tiny scoreboard with the name of the best glass destroyer.

UT2004 Convoy

Game version: Shack RC
Gamemodes: Push (modern)
Size: 30 MB
Release date: 13. 9. 2022
Download: here
Unique features:
- Intro cutscene with voiceover
- Objective announcer (yes, custom sounds!)
- Interactive objectives that change the layout of the map
- Entire match happens on top of or inside massive vehicles driving through the desert

The plot is a basically a scenario where NEG special forces (space marines) try to recapture NEXUS missiles (nukes) stolen by Hellions (space prirates).

I took a liberty of moving defender spawns so that defenders actually spawn in the same spot the whole match - the map layout changes for them as the attackers progress through the map.
